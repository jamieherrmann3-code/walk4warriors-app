Walk 4 Warriors Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 31, 2026

Walk 4 Warriors respects your privacy. This application does not collect, store, or share personal information.

Location Information

The app may access your device location while you use the walk or ruck tracking feature. Location data is used only to calculate distance and display progress during an activity.

Location data remains on your device and is not transmitted to or stored by Walk 4 Warriors, VFW Post 1318, or the app developer.

Data Collection

Walk 4 Warriors does not require account creation and does not collect, store, sell, or share personal information.

Third-Party Services

The app may provide links to third-party websites such as event registration or social media pages. Any information provided on those websites is subject to their respective privacy policies.
